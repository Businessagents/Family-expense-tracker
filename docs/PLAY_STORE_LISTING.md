## Full Description (4000 characters max)

```
Family Expense Tracker is the easiest way to manage shared expenses with your family. Whether you're splitting grocery bills, tracking household utilities, or managing vacation costs, our app makes it simple to stay on top of your family's finances.

KEY FEATURES

Shared Expense Tracking
Add expenses and automatically split them among family members. Categorize spending with customizable categories including Groceries, Utilities, Rent, Transport, Entertainment, Healthcare, and more.

Family Groups
Create or join family groups with a simple invite code. See who paid for what and track everyone's contributions in real-time.

Smart Balance Calculations
Automatically calculates who owes whom. View individual balances and settle up with a tap. No more confusion about shared expenses.

Powerful Analytics
Visualize your family's spending with beautiful charts:
- Monthly spending trends
- Category breakdown
- Member contributions
- Daily expense patterns

Secure Authentication
Protect your financial data with:
- 4-6 digit PIN login
- Fingerprint/Face ID support
- Auto-lock after inactivity
- Secure encrypted storage

Export & Reports
Export your expense data to CSV format for record-keeping, tax purposes, or import into spreadsheet applications.

Multi-Currency Support
Track expenses in multiple currencies including USD, INR, CAD, and SAR.

PERFECT FOR

- Families sharing household expenses
- Roommates splitting bills
- Couples managing joint finances
- Travel groups tracking trip costs
- Anyone who shares expenses with others

WHY CHOOSE FAMILY EXPENSE TRACKER?

Simple & Intuitive - Clean design that anyone can use
Private & Secure - Your financial data stays private
No Ads - Focus on what matters without distractions
Free to Use - All core features available at no cost
Works Offline - Log expenses anywhere, sync when connected

Download Family Expense Tracker today and take control of your family's finances!
```
